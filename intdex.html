Success Strategy

Focus on Logic (60%)

The majority of marks come from correct conditional branching (e.g., Even/Odd check, Carry check) and loop control.

Initialization Matters (20%)

Don't forget to set up your counters and accumulators before entering the loop. R16 = 0, R17 = 0.

Documentation (10%)

Marks are easy to lose here. Ensure you have a flowchart and comments explaining why you are using specific instructions.
